FAQ's

How does the exchange program work?

  1. The exchange program is facilitated by EV Mart's exchange partners on behalf of EV Mart. Please refer to the terms and conditions related to the exchange process.
  2. Once you provide the details of your old vehicle via the EV Mart digital purchase journey, an exchange value for your vehicle will be determined.
  3. You can confirm to apply the determined exchange value as discount and make payment of the discounted price to confirm your order.
  4. The exchange partner’s team will coordinate with you to inspect the vehicle at your premises or the address provided by you.
  5. If the inspection is successful, you have to submit the original documents pertaining to your old vehicle. Post the successful verification of documents, your vehicle will be picked up.

Which vehicles are eligible for exchange?

The vehicle models allowed for exchange are configured in the EV Mart digital purchase journey. You will be able to see the applicable models based on the pincode of registration.

What documents need to be submitted?

  1. The Customer must ensure that the details provided at the time of making the purchase with exchange (make, model, variant, manufacturing year, km driven, RC number) match the details of the vehicle at the time of inspection.
  2. The Customer must also provide the original registration certificate, valid insurance document, picture of the exchange vehicle, KYC documents of the registered owner of the vehicle and any other document requested by the exchange partner’s team
  3. For vehicles purchased on loan, the loan must be cleared and a valid NOC (No Objection Certificate) from the financier is needed to remove hypothecation.
  4. The customer must provide a surrender letter for the Exchange Vehicle and sign all the relevant documents/forms required under law for the transfer of the ownership / removal of hypothecation as part of the exchange process under the applicable law.
